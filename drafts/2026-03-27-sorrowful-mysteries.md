In this episode of St. Helena Ministries – Daily Rosary, we prayerfully meditate on the Sorrowful Mysteries, inviting you to enter more deeply into the passion of Christ through contemplative prayer. Each mystery is thoughtfully presented to guide your heart and mind in union with the sufferings of our Lord as revealed in the Gospels, particularly in the accounts found in the books of Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John. This time of prayer offers a peaceful moment to reflect on Christ’s love and sacrifice with reverence and quiet devotion.

If you find this ministry a source of spiritual nourishment, we gently invite you to support our work so that we may continue to share these daily prayers with the wider community. Your generosity helps sustain this humble apostolate.

For those seeking to enrich their daily prayer life further, we also encourage you to explore our Divine Office podcast, which offers the Church’s Liturgy of the Hours, helping you join in the universal prayer of the Church throughout the day.

May this prayerful time bring you closer to the heart of Christ and deepen your peace in Him.
