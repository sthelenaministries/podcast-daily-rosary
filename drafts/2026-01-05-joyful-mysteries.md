In this episode of the St. Helena Ministries – Daily Rosary, we prayerfully meditate on the Joyful Mysteries, inviting you to join in reflecting on the early life of Christ and the grace-filled moments of His earthly journey. Each decade is prayed with reverence, fostering a deeper connection to the mysteries that reveal God’s loving plan. As you listen and pray along, may your heart be drawn closer to the peace and joy found in the Gospel accounts of the Annunciation, Visitation, Nativity, Presentation, and Finding in the Temple (Luke 1; Luke 2).
