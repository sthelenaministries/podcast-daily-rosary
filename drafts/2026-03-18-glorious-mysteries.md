In this episode of St. Helena Ministries – Daily Rosary, we gather in prayer to meditate on the Glorious Mysteries, inviting the faithful to reflect on the Resurrection of Our Lord, the Ascension, the Descent of the Holy Spirit, the Assumption of Mary, and the Coronation of the Blessed Virgin. Through these sacred mysteries, we enter more deeply into the Paschal Mystery and the hope of eternal life as revealed in the Gospel of Luke, Acts, and Revelation. Join us in this calm and reverent prayer, allowing the Rosary to guide your heart closer to Christ and His Mother.
