In this episode of the St. Helena Ministries – Daily Rosary, we prayerfully meditate on the Joyful Mysteries, inviting you to enter into the joyful moments of Our Lord’s early life with Mary and Joseph. Through this sacred prayer, we reflect on the Annunciation, the Visitation, the Nativity, the Presentation in the Temple, and the Finding of Jesus in the Temple, drawing closer to the mysteries of faith as revealed in the Gospel of Luke. Join us in this calm and reverent recitation, allowing the Rosary to deepen your daily communion with Christ and His Blessed Mother.
