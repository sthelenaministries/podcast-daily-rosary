In this episode of the St. Helena Ministries Daily Rosary, we gather in prayer to meditate on the Joyful Mysteries, inviting the presence of Our Lady to guide our hearts through the profound moments of Christ’s early life. As we contemplate these sacred events, we join a timeless tradition of devotion that nurtures faith and deepens our relationship with God. This prayerful time offers a peaceful space to reflect on the mysteries revealed in the Gospel, including passages from Luke and Matthew, fostering a spirit of reverence and quiet contemplation.
